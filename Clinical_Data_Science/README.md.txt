# Clinical Data Science Portfolio

This section contains clinical data science projects focused on
real-world healthcare problems including disease phenotyping,
clinical outcome prediction, and clinical text analysis.

## Key Skills Demonstrated
- Clinical data preprocessing
- Text mining and NLP
- Phenotyping using structured data
- Outcome prediction models
- Clinical data interpretation

## Tools & Technologies
- SQL
- R
- Excel
- Clinical datasets (EHR-based, de-identified)

## Background
These projects are derived from academic coursework, clinical training,
and independent analytics projects, aligned with healthcare and
clinical research applications.
