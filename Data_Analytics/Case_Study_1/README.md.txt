# Cyclistic Bike-Share Case Study

## Objective
To analyze how annual members and casual riders use Cyclistic bike-share services differently and provide business recommendations to convert casual riders into annual members.

## Dataset
Public bike-share trip data provided by Cyclistic (Divvy open data).
Time period: 12 months of ride data.

## Tools Used
- Excel (data inspection and cleaning)
- SQL (data filtering and aggregation)
- R (data analysis and visualization using tidyverse and ggplot2)

## Data Cleaning
- Removed rides with negative or zero duration
- Standardized date and time formats
- Removed missing station names
- Created ride_length and day_of_week variables

## Analysis Summary
- Casual riders use bikes more on weekends
- Annual members show consistent weekday usage
- Casual riders prefer longer ride durations

## Business Recommendations
- Introduce weekend membership offers
- Promote leisure-based ride packages
- Target casual riders with conversion campaigns
